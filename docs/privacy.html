On My Radar

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Last Updated: July 2025

Privacy Overview

On My Radar is designed with your privacy in mind. The app is fully open source, allowing you to inspect the code and verify our privacy commitments. It operates entirely on your local device without any network connectivity or data collection.

Data Collection

On My Radar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics are tracked
  • No crash reports are sent
  • No network requests are made
  • No third-party services are integrated
  • No advertising or tracking SDKs are included

Data Storage

All tasks and preferences are stored locally on your Mac using standard macOS storage mechanisms. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not synchronized to any cloud service
  • Is not accessible to the developer or any third party
  • Can be deleted at any time by removing the app

Permissions

On My Radar requires no special permissions to operate. The app:

  • Does not require accessibility permissions
  • Does not access your camera or microphone
  • Does not access your location
  • Does not access your contacts or calendar
  • Operates entirely within the macOS sandbox for your security

Open Source Transparency

On My Radar is open source software. You can:

  • View the complete source code on GitHub
  • Verify our privacy claims by examining the code
  • Build the app yourself from source
  • Contribute to the project or report issues

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the app's release notes and on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

This privacy policy is effective as of the date stated above and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future, which will be in effect immediately after being posted on this page.
