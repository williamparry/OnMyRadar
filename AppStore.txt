Description (4000 characters max)
On My Radar is the task manager that lives where you work – right in your menu bar. No more switching between apps or losing focus. Your tasks are always just one click away.

DESIGNED FOR FOCUS
• Clean, minimal interface that stays out of your way
• Quick task entry with instant save
• Zero loading time – your tasks appear instantly
• Transparent background option to maintain your flow

SMART STATUS TRACKING
Track your tasks with three simple states:
• On Me – Tasks you're actively working on
• Waiting – Tasks blocked or awaiting response  
• Done – Completed tasks for that satisfaction boost

BUILT FOR PRODUCTIVITY
• Global keyboard shortcut for instant access
• Click to edit tasks inline
• Visual status indicators at a glance
• Customizable status labels to match your workflow

PERSONALIZE YOUR EXPERIENCE
• Choose between symbols or text for status display
• Adjust panel transparency
• Configure your preferred keyboard shortcut
• Tailor status labels to your terminology

PRIVACY FIRST
• All data stored locally on your Mac
• No cloud services or internet connection required
• Your tasks never leave your device
• Complete privacy and data ownership
• Open source - verify our privacy claims on GitHub

NATIVE PERFORMANCE
Built with SwiftUI for optimal macOS integration:
• Minimal CPU and memory usage
• Instant response times
• Battery-efficient operation
• Seamless macOS experience

Perfect for developers, designers, writers, and anyone who values a clean workspace and efficient task management. On My Radar keeps your tasks visible without cluttering your screen or disrupting your workflow.

Your tasks, your way, always on your radar.
